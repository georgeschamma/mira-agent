# Media Plan Strategy Narrative Guidelines

These guidelines assist the strategy node in generating the final media plan document.

## Tone & Style
- **Authoritative**: Write from the perspective of a seasoned B2B media strategist.
- **Sourced**: Base all recommendations on facts in the strategic brief, GA4 performance summaries, research findings, and CRM segments.
- **Actionable**: Frame recommendations around testable hypotheses, KPIs, and clear execution steps.

## Anti-Patterns (NEVER DO THESE)
- **Do NOT invent fitted-channel spend**: Spend numbers for GA4-fitted channels must match the deterministic table exactly.
- **Do NOT recommend scaling saturated channels**: If a channel is saturated and has mROI below 0.05, explain why we are holding or capping it, and do not recommend increases.
- **Do NOT ignore the expansion budget**: If `expansion_budget` is greater than 0, the Executive Summary and Expansion sections must explicitly explain where that budget goes and how it is divided among recommended tests.
- **Do NOT write generic placeholder text**: Avoid sentences like "optimize channels to maximize conversions" without citing specific numbers or channels.
- **Do NOT miss source claims**: Ensure all claims are properly cited using allowable prefixes (`https://`, `brief:`, `crm:segment:`, `ga4:`, `performance:`).
